Engineering Safety for Modern Infrastructure

Autowheel delivers reliable road safety and industrial safety equipment designed to perform in real-world conditions. About Us

AutoWheel India is a professionally managed organization dedicated to delivering comprehensive vehicle safety, testing, and compliance. With a strong focus onquality, accuracy, and regulatory adherence, we support safer roads and efficient vehicle performance across India. Our operations span across vehicle testing infrastructure, wheelcare services, road safety equipment, and pollution control equipment. All products andsystems supplied by Auto Wheel India are in accordance with the norms, guidelines, and standards prescribed by the Central Government of India and relevant regulatory authorities.

Automated Testing Station (ATS) Machinery Supplier

AutoWheel India is a trusted supplier and distributor of Automated Testing Station(ATS) machinery used for vehicle fitness testing. We supply MoRTH-approved ATS equipment, designed and configured strictly as per Central Government norms and specifications. Our ATS equipment support accurate, transparent, and automated inspectionof vehicles, helping testing centers, transport authorities, and private operators comply withstatutory vehicle fitness requirements. We emphasize reliability, precision, and long-termoperational efficiency in all ATS machinery supplied.

Wheel alignment equipment supplier

We provide professional wheel alignment and balancing equipment to enhance vehicle safety, handling, and tire life. Using advanced equipment and precise calibration methods,our services help ensure optimal vehicle performance and driving comfort. Proper wheelalignment not only improves fuel efficiency but also reduces uneven tire wear, contributing tosafer and more economical vehicle operation.

Road Safety Products

AutoWheel India supplies a wide range of road safety products essential for traffic management and accident prevention. Our product portfolio includes safety cones,barricades, reflective jackets, delineators, and other traffic safety equipment. All road safetyproducts are manufactured and supplied as per applicable government standards and safety norms, ensuring durability, visibility, and effectiveness for use on highways, urbanroads, construction zones, and testing facilities.

PUC test machinery supplier

We also offer reliable PUC testing machinery to support vehicle emission monitoring and environmental compliance. Our PUC systems are designed to meet regulatory requirements and help reduce vehicular pollution through accurate testing and reporting. By enabling compliance with emission norms, we contribute to cleaner air and sustainable transportation practices.

Our Commitment

At AutoWheel India, we are committed to delivering standard-compliant products,technical expertise, and dependable service. Our goal is to support government bodies,private operators, and vehicle owners by providing equipment that enhance road safety,regulatory compliance, and overall transportation efficiency
